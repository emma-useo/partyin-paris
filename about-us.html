The little story

Erwann, Emma et Léo, 3 students that wanted to bought their tiquets for the biggest event of the year in Paris. Places were taken up very quickly, and the time the 3 students took to find the event page and the ticketing, the event was sold out. They made the conclusion that it should exist a way to know about an event and to book the tiquet much more easily. So they created Party’n Paris.
